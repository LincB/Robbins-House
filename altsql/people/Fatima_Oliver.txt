Fatima Oliver~1~1~5~First wife of Peter Robbins. There is no record that Peter and Fatima Robbins had any children. In 1837, Fatima sued Peter for divorce. By this point he had fathered five children by Almira Oliver Davis. She was granted \"one full third part of the real estate formerly owned by sad Peter Robbins and now occupied by him.\"~~~~~~~~~~~*Peter Robbins~10~~~~*
