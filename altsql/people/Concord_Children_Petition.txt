Concord Children Petition~1~1~7~An antislavery petition, written by Mary Rice, that was signed by the children of Concord, and sent to the white house. The heading was written by Mary Mann so when President Lincoln recieved he wrote back the letter to Mary Mann not, which who should have got the credit, Mary Rice.~~*Mary Rice~6~~~~~*Mary Mann~8~~~~~*Abraham Lincoln~10~~~~*
