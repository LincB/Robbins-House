John Brown~1~1~7~On October 16, 1859, he led 21 men on a raid of the federal arsenal at Harpers Ferry, Virginia.  Spoke in Concord three times to raise funds for his activities in Kansas and elsewhere.  After his execution, Thoreau was the first public figure to speak publicly in his defense (\"Plea for Captain John Brown\" ).
